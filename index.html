Welcome to my website!

For many years I have been a huge fan of all things My Little Pony. My first introduction to the show was the 3rd generation, with DVD specials such as, The Princess Promenade and, my all time favourite, A Very Minty Christmas. It was no surprise that when My Little Pony: Friendship is Magic rolled around, I was enamoured. It quickly became my favourite show, and as a child, I made my mom 'tape' every new episode. I was amazed by The Mane 6's unique personalities and the epic adventures they went on.

I have held on to many of my figurines and DVD's, and continue to collect more on occasion. I have also continued to make art of these colourful little horses, nearly rivaling the amount I made as a child. I hope you enjoy this website as an ongoing archive of my interest.
